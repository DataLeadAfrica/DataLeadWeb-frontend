Methodology · How We Got Here

No guesswork. A model.

Every prediction that follows comes from the same transparent pipeline. Nothing is hand-picked.

1

Rate

Each team gets a strength rating anchored to its FIFA ranking, then adjusted for observed 2026 form (goal margins, opponent quality).

2

Simulate

Every knockout match is resolved by an Elo win-probability formula. The full bracket is played out 400,000 times.

3

Aggregate

A team's odds are simply the share of simulations in which it reaches each stage. Probabilities, not opinions.

The Model · Team Ratings

The sixteen, ranked

FIFA ranking is the anchor; the model rating adds current form; the simulation turns it into title probability and fair odds.
